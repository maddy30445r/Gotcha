Gotcha

For developers in Hinglish standups

Just say Gotcha.
We’ll handle the meeting.

Hindi-English standups move fast and leads talk in hints. Gotcha is your friendly meeting advisor: it listens when you say so, separates your voice from theirs, and hands back the decision, what your lead really meant, and your next move — each one with the receipt. No more guessing or re-prompting an AI after the call.

  1. Recordthe call, two tracks — you and them, kept apart
  2. Decodethe decisions and what was really meant
  3. Acton your items, each backed by the exact moment

why developers keep it open

  • ✶

    A friendly advisor, not a transcript

    Gotcha decodes the hard parts — what was decided, what your lead meant, and exactly what you now owe — and tells you your next move.

  • ▶

    Points you to the exact moment

    Every claim is a tappable timestamp that jumps the audio to that second. Recheck the important bits in seconds, not by re-listening.

  • ●

    Only listens when you allow it

    No always-on mic, no 24/7 surveillance. Gotcha records only between your Record and Stop — and nothing else.

  • ◇

    Lightweight & out of your way

    A small, fast app that sits quietly until you need it — not a heavy, bloated client hogging your machine.

  • 🔒

    Private by design

    Your audio is encrypted in transit and at rest, never used to train any AI model, and you can wipe your data anytime.
