Install & first run

  1. Open the downloaded Gotcha.dmg and drag Gotcha to Applications.
  2. First launch only: right-click → Open (the build is self-signed, not yet notarized), then confirm.
  3. When asked, grant Microphone and Screen Recording — Gotcha needs both to capture you and the call as separate tracks. After granting Screen Recording the app relaunches itself.
  4. Click Open in Gotcha above (or paste your server + token in ⚙ Settings), then hit ● Record when your meeting starts.

Privacy

  • On-demand only. Gotcha records solely while you hold a meeting and press Record — there is no background or always-on listening.
  • Managed keys. Transcription and analysis run on Gotcha's backend over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection; your audio and reports are stored on an encrypted disk in your own namespace.
  • You control your data. Recordings stay tied to your account; nothing is shared across users.
  • Alpha note: during the alpha, interpretation uses a model tier that may use inputs to improve the service — please record non-confidential / test meetings only until the no-train tier is enabled.
